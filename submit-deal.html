Buying Criteria

Our Acquisition Criteria

Here's what you need to know before submitting a deal.

🏝️

Residential: Southwest Florida

Our residential buying area covers Lee County, Collier County, and Charlotte County. Fort Myers, Cape Coral, Naples, Lehigh Acres, Bonita Springs, Estero, and surrounding areas. Any price range, any condition, any situation. We buy single-family, small multifamily, condos, townhouses, and vacant land.

🌎

Commercial: All 50 States

Our commercial acquisitions are nationwide. We buy storage, mobile parks, multifamily, industrial, office, and retail in every state. No geographic restriction on commercial deals. Any deal size, any condition, stabilized or value-add.

⏱

24-Hour Response

Every deal submission gets a response within 24 hours. If we're interested, you'll receive a preliminary offer or a request for additional information. If it's not a fit, we'll tell you straight — no ghosting, no weeks of silence.

💰

We Close Fast

Residential: 7–21 days. Commercial: 14–45 days. We provide proof of funds on request and don't re-trade after going under contract. We can double close or work with assignable contracts — whatever structure works for the deal.

🔄

Double Close or Assign

We're comfortable with assignments, double closings, and direct purchases. If you have an assignable contract, we can step in as the end buyer. If you're marketing a property you control, we can close directly. We're flexible on deal structure.

📈

All Price Ranges

We don't have a minimum or maximum deal size. A $50,000 wholesale deal gets the same attention and response time as a $5 million commercial acquisition. Good deals come in all sizes.

Why Partner With Us

Why Wholesalers and Agents Work With Transition Properties

We're the buyer you want on the other end of your deal. Here's why.

⏱

We Respond Fast

Every submission gets a response within 24 hours — not days, not "when we get to it." We know your deal has a timeline, and we respect that. If it's a fit, you'll know immediately. If it's not, we'll tell you right away so you can move on.

🤝

We Actually Close

When we go under contract, we close. We don't tie up your deal for weeks and then walk away. We provide proof of funds upfront, close on the agreed date, and don't re-trade. Our reputation as a reliable closer is the foundation of our business.

💬

We're Easy to Work With

No ego, no drama, no unnecessary complexity. We communicate clearly, make decisions quickly, and treat every person we work with — from first-time bird dogs to seasoned commercial brokers — with the same respect and professionalism.

🔄

Repeat Business

We're not looking for one deal — we're looking for ongoing relationships with reliable deal sources. If you bring us good deals consistently, we'll prioritize your submissions and build a long-term partnership that benefits both sides.

📋

Transparent Communication

You'll always know where your deal stands. We don't go dark during due diligence or leave you wondering. If there's an issue, we flag it early. If we're moving forward, you'll know the timeline. Clear, honest communication at every step.

💰

We Pay Referral Fees

Bird dogs and referral sources who bring us deals that close get paid. If you don't have the deal under contract but you know about a property, reach out anyway. We'll discuss a referral fee structure that works for both of us.
