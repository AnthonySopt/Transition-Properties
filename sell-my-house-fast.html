Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Honest answers to the questions we hear most from homeowners considering a fast cash sale.

How do I sell my house fast in Southwest Florida?

The fastest way to sell your house in Southwest Florida is to work with a direct cash buyer like Transition Properties. Simply contact us by phone at (239) 766-6978 or fill out the form at the top of this page. We'll evaluate your property and present a no-obligation cash offer within 24 hours. If you accept, we can close in as few as 7 days. There are no showings, no open houses, and no waiting for a buyer's financing to come through.

What is a fair cash offer for my house?

A fair cash offer takes into account your property's location, current condition, necessary repairs, and comparable recent sales in the neighborhood. Our offers are typically below full retail value because we buy as-is and take on all repair risk — but the trade-off is speed, certainty, and zero out-of-pocket costs for you. We're fully transparent about how we calculate our offers, and you're never under any obligation to accept.

Do cash home buyers charge fees or commissions?

No. Transition Properties charges absolutely zero fees and zero commissions. There are no agent commissions (we're not realtors), no closing cost charges on your end, and no hidden administrative fees. The amount we offer is the amount you receive. This is one of the biggest advantages of selling directly to a cash buyer instead of listing on the open market.

How fast can I close on my house sale?

We can close in as little as 7 days from the time you accept our offer. That said, we work entirely on your schedule. If you need 30 or up to 60 days, that's perfectly fine. Some homeowners need time to find a new place, wrap up an estate, or coordinate a move. You pick the closing date and we'll meet you there.

Do I need to make repairs before selling to an investor?

Absolutely not. We buy houses in any condition — damaged roofs, foundation problems, mold, fire damage, hoarding situations, outdated systems, code violations, and everything in between. You don't need to clean, stage, or fix a single thing. We handle all of that after closing.

What happens after I request a cash offer?

After you fill out the form or call us, here's what to expect: within 24 hours, we'll reach out to discuss your property and situation. We'll then prepare a written cash offer based on our evaluation. You review the offer on your own time — no pressure, no expiration tricks. If it works for you, we open with a reputable local title company and you choose your closing date. On closing day, you sign the paperwork and receive your cash. That's the entire process.
